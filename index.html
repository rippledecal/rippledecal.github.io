Welcome to the Ripple DeCal! Ripple is an open source payment protocol for any store of value. Payments on the decentralized Ripple network are instant, irreversible, and free. With Ripple there are endless possibilities to make the world of financial transactions cheaper, faster, and more accessible for everyone.

This semester you will be learning all about the open source Ripple Protocol and building your own project on top of it. And you won’t be alone. The semester will be filled with guest lectures and mentorship from the Ripple Labs team as well as the partners of CrossCoin Ventures. Come learn about the open source protocol that is out to bring the world of financial transactions into the 21st century.
